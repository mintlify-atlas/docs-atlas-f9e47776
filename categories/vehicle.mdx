---

Vehicle OSINT encompasses a range of lookup services that help investigators and researchers identify vehicles, verify ownership claims, and uncover the history behind a specific car or truck. VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) history checks reveal accident records, title brands, odometer readings, and recall information — useful for fraud investigations and due diligence. License plate lookups can surface registration records and vehicle make/model information. These services are used in insurance investigations, background research, law enforcement support, and physical security operations where vehicle identification plays a role.

<Note>
  Availability of vehicle lookup services varies significantly by region. Services like FaxVin, EpicVin, and AutoCheck are primarily US-focused and draw from US title and registration records. The GOV.UK tool is UK-specific, pulling data from the DVLA. PlatesMania and auto-info.gratis offer broader international coverage for registration plate research.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Vehicle lookup APIs must comply with the Driver's Privacy Protection Act (DPPA) in the US. Only use vehicle data for lawful purposes — permissible uses are defined by the DPPA and include legitimate business, legal, research, and law enforcement purposes.
</Warning>
