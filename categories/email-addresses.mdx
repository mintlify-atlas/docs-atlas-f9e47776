Email OSINT is a foundational skill for both offensive and defensive security work. From discovering corporate email formats used by a target organization to verifying whether a specific address is live, these tools cover the full spectrum. Use them to uncover email addresses tied to a domain, validate deliverability before outreach, check sender reputation for phishing analysis, and investigate whether addresses have been exposed in data breaches. Many tools combine multiple capabilities — email discovery, format detection, and verification — in a single workflow.

<Tip>
  **Hunter.io** is the go-to starting point for corporate email discovery. Combine with **EmailHippo** or **Reacher.email** for deliverability verification.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Email lookup tools are subject to GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and other regional privacy regulations. Many of these services aggregate publicly available data, but using them to harvest email addresses for unsolicited contact or mass marketing may be unlawful. Always ensure your use case is compliant with applicable law and the terms of service of each platform. Treat personal email addresses discovered through OSINT with the same care as other personally identifiable information (PII).
</Note>
